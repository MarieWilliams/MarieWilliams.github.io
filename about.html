About

Marie lives and works in Nottinghamshire, since graduating in 2014 from the University of Nottingham with a Degree in Fine Art she has continued to develop her practice from her studio at home. The 'fragility of memory' continues to inform her drawings, and the relationships created between these individual pieces.
