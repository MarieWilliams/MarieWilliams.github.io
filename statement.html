Artist Statement

My work examines the recurring theme of memory, the way in which we piece together fragments of information. Key elements of my work are domestic objects within interior spaces - I see them as evoking a sense of place, lived experiences both happy and sad.

I have concentrated my observations through the continuation of drawing. Graph and lined paper are my preferred surfaces and marks have been made by drawing blind onto carbon paper. This process leaves intended marks and others by chance which creates an uncertain outcome, variables that are out of my control. Heavily influenced by childhood these drawings are a direct response to the 'fragility of memory'.
